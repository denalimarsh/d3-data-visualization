About

This interactive sankey particle flow visualization shows the top 20 refugees flows of 2010 in absolute figures. Region is encoded by color, allowing us to see from which parts of the world refugees originate as well as which region formally accepts their refugee application. While most of the flow values (hover over a connecting path to see the refugee count) are exact, the two largest (Iraq to Syrian Arab Republic and Iraq to Jordan) are given in approximate values as few reliable statistics can be recovered from 2010 war torn Iraq. Select and drag country bars to move the location of the countries and get different perspectives on the flow of refugees.
